Uthao Cargo Movement Process

Discover how we orchestrate global ocean freight with precision, transparency, and care at every single nautical mile.

Book Shipment

Start your journey through our intuitive digital dashboard. Our platform allows you to compare routes, get instant quotes, and secure space on passenger or intermodal shipping in a simple, guided experience.

Pack, Label & Collect

Once loaded, our team of vans prepares the cargo for its ocean voyage. Utilizing digital inventory tracking, every pallet and box is given a unique RFID-embedded label. Our professionals then collect the goods, ensuring they are properly secured for the initial leg of the journey to the consolidation center.

Export & Loading

At the port of origin, your cargo meets the massive scale of ocean logistics. High-impact gantry cranes precisely lift and stack containers according to complex weight distribution plans. Our on-site agents verify every piece against manifest data, ensuring your goods are prioritized and placed securely for the crossing.

Ocean Transit

During the voyage, Uthao provides unparalleled visibility. Our satellite-linked tracking systems provide real-time updates on your vessel’s position, speed, and environmental conditions. We monitor global weather patterns and can coordinate a proactively manage any potential delays before they impact your supply chain.

Import & Delivery

As the vessel reaches its destination, our customs clearance experts handle the final regulatory hurdles. We manage all duties, taxes, and import licenses to ensure a smooth transition. Finally, a dedicated delivery vehicle completes the “last mile,” bringing your cargo safely to its final destination, whether it’s a warehouse, retail store, or factory.
